About Me

I'm currently a post-doctoral researcher in Urban Resilience, Networks, and Informatics Lab in Zachry Department of Civil Engineering at Texas A&M University (TAMU) . I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Civil Engineering with specialization in Transportation Engineering, minor in Computer Science from Oregon State University (OSU). I received Dual B.E. in Information and Computational Science from School of Mathematical Sciences, and Finance from School and School of Management and Economics at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2013.

My interdisciplinary research is at the nexus of network sciences, infrastructure engineering, and social science. My research topics include infrastructure network resilience analysis, societal impact of infrastructure failure, smart & resilient city, disaster informatics, and resilience planning & management.
